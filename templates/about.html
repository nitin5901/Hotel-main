Welcome to Hotel Navdurga

Hotel Navdurga is a deluxe restaurant located in Aau village. We are conveniently located in the downtown of Jodhpur Near Jaipur-Jaiselmer Highway. Our hotel is at mid-way of the highway , while going to Jaiselmer we will provide you with best food and accomodation. Our location is just perfect for the travelers interested in shopping as we are near the Aau markets.

We offer a classic mix of luxurious comfort, reasonable pricing and beautiful nature. Navdurga Hotel has a quiet, peaceful and posh locality free from air and noise pollution.

Our restaurant is open for breakfast, lunch and dinner to serve you. While we present a broad range of traveler and global favorites, we specialize in traditional Rajasthan cuisine. A great many vegetarian options are also available. 360 view of the city is available from our restaurant.
